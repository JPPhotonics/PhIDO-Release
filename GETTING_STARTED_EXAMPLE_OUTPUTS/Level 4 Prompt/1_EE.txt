  MMIs arranged in a four-stage tree formation. Each of the 16 outputs is then connected
  to a variable optical attenuator (VOA), a thermo-optic phase shifter, and finally
  to a grating coupler.

  process with each subsequent stage of MMIs until there are 16 outputs. Each output
  should connect to a VOA, then a thermo-optic phase shifter, and finally to a grating
  coupler.'
